MS/PhD in Medical Biophysics

Program Description:
The MS/PhD programs in Medical Biophysics are research/career oriented graduate programs that prepare students for careers in academia, government institutions and bio-oriented enterprises. The students are introduced to all fundamental aspects of Medical Biophysics which allows them to further specialise in any area of bio-medical research and manufacturing. For further information, contact Prof. Emil Alexov: ealexov@clemson.edu
